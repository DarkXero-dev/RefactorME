# Refactor.me, 25 design directions (Phase 0 divergence)

Generated to break the anchor before building. No two share a colour philosophy, a
font pairing, or a layout skeleton. None is the forbidden terminal/code-editor look
(near-black + single bright accent + monospace). **Build #1 (Gintsugi) unless told
otherwise.** The other 24 exist to force divergence and to give the operator choices.

---

1. **Gintsugi (LOCKED, build this)**. Palette: deep cool indigo/aubergine/slate glaze
   dark, lit only by precious silver/platinum. Type: Cormorant display, Manrope body.
   Layout: calm editorial gallery, silver seams as connective tissue down the page.
   Signature: a cracked ceramic tile holding the ugly site; the fractures fill with
   luminous silver and the site becomes clean. Repair as art.

2. **Risograph Workshop**. Palette: newsprint cream with fluoro pink and electric blue
   duotone, visible misregistration. Type: Darker Grotesque display, Karla body. Layout:
   offset print layers, registration marks in margins. Signature: two off-register riso
   layers snap into perfect registration as before becomes after.

3. **Cyanotype Blueprint**. Palette: deep prussian-blue paper, white line ink only.
   Type: Spectral drafting serif with technical small-caps labels. Layout: drafting
   sheet with a title block and dimension lines. Signature: the site is drafted as a
   white blueprint, then "constructed" into a rendered page.

4. **Botanical Herbarium**. Palette: warm pressed-paper, sage, sepia ink. Type: EB
   Garamond display, Spectral body. Layout: specimen-card grid with handwritten labels
   and tape. Signature: a wilted, browned plant specimen revives and blooms.

5. **Swiss International**. Palette: pure white, one red, black. Type: Archivo, tight
   and large. Layout: strict 12-column grid, massive headlines, ruthless alignment.
   Signature: a chaotic skewed layout snaps every element onto the grid.

6. **Brutalist Concrete**. Palette: raw concrete grey, hazard yellow, black. Type:
   Archivo Black display, system body. Layout: exposed boxes, visible borders, no
   radius. Signature: a glitching, torn before slams into hard clean blocks.

7. **Art Deco Gilded**. Palette: lacquer black, emerald, champagne. Type: Cinzel
   display, Poiret One body. Layout: symmetrical, fan and sunburst motifs, gilt frames.
   Signature: a deco frame assembles itself around the mended site.

8. **Vaporwave Arcade**. Palette: sunset magenta to teal gradient, chrome. Type:
   Outfit with chrome treatment. Layout: perspective grid horizon, floating busts.
   Signature: a dead 90s page powers on into glowing synth chrome.

9. **Frutiger Aero Glass**. Palette: glossy aqua, sky, white, water droplets. Type:
   Quicksand. Layout: floating frosted-glass panels, lens bloom. Signature: a dull flat
   page inflates into glossy 3D glass bubbles.

10. **Letterpress Broadsheet**. Palette: ink black on warm off-white, one spot. Type:
    Old Standard TT display, Source Serif body, drop caps. Layout: dense multi-column
    broadsheet with hairline rules. Signature: a smudged proof press becomes crisp.

11. **Terrazzo Pastel**. Palette: mint, peach, lilac speckle on cream. Type: Fredoka
    display, Nunito body. Layout: rounded blobs, playful asymmetry. Signature: a cracked
    terrazzo slab is re-poured smooth with new chips.

12. **Bauhaus Primary**. Palette: red, blue, yellow, black on white. Type: Archivo
    geometric. Layout: modular shape blocks, circles and triangles. Signature: scattered
    primitive shapes reassemble into the page layout.

13. **Cyber-Noir Rain**. Palette: wet-asphalt charcoal, magenta and cyan neon (multi,
    cinematic, not single-accent), rain streaks. Type: Saira Condensed. Layout:
    widescreen cinematic bands. Signature: a flickering broken neon sign steadies.

14. **Scandinavian Hygge**. Palette: oat, dusty blue, soft clay. Type: Fraunces display,
    Reddit Sans body. Layout: cozy, generous, warm light. Signature: a cluttered shelf
    tidies itself into calm order.

15. **Fashion Editorial Didone**. Palette: stark white, jet, one ink. Type: Bodoni Moda
    oversized. Layout: asymmetric magazine spreads, huge negative space. Signature: a
    messy contact sheet resolves into one cover shot.

16. **Origami Paper**. Palette: folded-paper pastels with crease shadows. Type:
    Quattrocento. Layout: faceted polygon panels. Signature: a crumpled sheet folds
    into a clean paper crane.

17. **Sumi-e Ink Wash**. Palette: rice-paper warm white, single indigo brush, vast void.
    Type: Shippori Mincho. Layout: vertical, extreme negative space, one seal stamp.
    Signature: a smeared muddy brushstroke resolves into one clean confident stroke.

18. **Stained Glass Cathedral**. Palette: leaded-glass jewel tones, cobalt, ruby, amber
    backlit on charcoal lead. Type: Cormorant SC display, Spectral body. Layout: mosaic
    grid of glass panes. Signature: dull grey glass lights up backlit and luminous.
    (Deliberately not the forbidden amber-terminal look: full jewel mosaic, not mono.)

19. **Memphis 80s**. Palette: confetti primaries plus pastel, squiggles. Type: Bungee
    display, Poppins body. Layout: chaotic sticker collage. Signature: a boring grey box
    bursts into confetti and squiggles.

20. **Monumental Concrete-Glass**. Palette: cool greys, vast photographic voids. Type:
    Hanken Grotesk, thin and monumental. Layout: huge whitespace, scaffolding rhythm.
    Signature: scaffolding falls away to reveal a glass facade.

21. **Sketchbook Marker**. Palette: paper white, graphite, one marker. Type: Caveat
    handwriting display, Karla body. Layout: doodled annotations and arrows. Signature:
    a napkin sketch inks itself into a finished UI.

22. **Baroque Restoration**. Palette: deep velvet plum, oxblood, antique cream. Type:
    Cardo display, Cormorant body, ornate. Layout: dense ornamental symmetry. Signature:
    a faded cracked fresco is cleaned and restored to vivid colour.

23. **Austere Mono-Ink**. Palette: off-white, single charcoal, nothing else. Type:
    Libre Franklin, tiny and precise. Layout: extreme restraint, one column, vast air.
    Signature: a single hairline redraws itself into the wordmark.

24. **Solarpunk Nouveau**. Palette: warm cream, leaf green, clay, brass curves. Type:
    Quintessential display, Mulish body. Layout: organic art-nouveau whiplash curves.
    Signature: a grey concrete block sprouts green growth across it.

25. **Liquid Chrome Y3K**. Palette: iridescent oil-slick metaliquid on black. Type:
    Unbounded display, Space Grotesk body. Layout: blobby 3D, soft chrome shapes.
    Signature: a pixelated low-res page melts and reflows into smooth liquid chrome.
