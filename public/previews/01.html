Fall asleep to a night train, not a loop.

Drift is a focus and sleep app built on real soundscapes: rain drumming a tin roof, a library at closing time, a slow train after midnight. No synthetic hum. Works fully offline, with a sleep timer that fades out and lets you go.

Recorded, not generated

Rain on a tin roof holds a rhythm no algorithm gets right. Drift captures the whole thing: the drip, the gust, the quiet gap before the next wave.

Download a soundscape once and it is on your phone for good. No signal on the train, in the cabin, at 30,000 feet: it plays anyway.

Set it for 20 minutes or two hours. Drift fades out slowly instead of cutting off, so the sound leaves before you notice it has gone.
