From cherry to cup

How your bag gets made

i

Farms we can name

We buy green beans from a handful of growers we visit and pay fairly, so we can tell you the farm, the altitude, and the hands that picked it.

ii

Roasted upstairs

Every batch is roasted in the room above the cafe on a cast-iron drum, watched by ear and nose, never more than a few kilos at a time.

iii

Bagged the same day

Beans rest overnight, then go straight into the bag with the roast date stamped on. No warehouse, no month-old stock, just coffee at its brightest.
