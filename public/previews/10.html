One record a week · since 1974 in spirit

A home for slow listening.

I play one record a week, all the way through, then write it up the next morning over coffee. No skipping, no shuffle. Just the whole side and whatever it leaves behind.

Start reading

How the week goes

Sunday night I pull one record off the shelf, drop the needle, and let it run start to finish. By Monday morning there are notes, and by the afternoon there is a write-up here.

01

The weekly write-up

Every Monday, one record gets a proper page: what it sounded like, where my head went on side two, and whether it earned a second spin. Written slow, on purpose.

02

The turntable setup

A hand-me-down belt-drive, a warm little valve amp, and two speakers that have seen better decades. Nothing audiophile about it. It just sounds like home.

03

Reader picks

The shelf is not just mine. Send me a record you love and it goes in the queue. Some of the best mornings started with a stranger's suggestion and a full pot of coffee.
